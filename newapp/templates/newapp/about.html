About Mypen

A space for writers and dreamers to share their stories with the world.

Our Mission

Mypen is a platform for writers and readers to connect and share stories, poems, and scripts. Our mission is to provide a clean, modern, and distraction-free environment for creators to showcase their work and for readers to discover new talent.

Whether you're writing a novel, a screenplay, or a short poem, Mypen gives you the tools you need to publish and manage your content with ease.
